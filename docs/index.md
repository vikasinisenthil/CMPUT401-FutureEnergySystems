# Future Energy Systems - Air Heroes
**The Quest for Clean Air**

## About

Meet the Air Heroes, a team dedicated to keeping our skies blue and our lungs healthy. They are traveling from the prairies to the coast to battle the "Smog Monsters / The Pollutants" and learn the secrets of the atmosphere.

## Characters

### Remi the Cyclist
**Bike Power! Zero tailpipe smoke.**

Abilities:

- Speed Boost: If the player lands on a "Ride Bike!" path or Green Spot, they move +1 extra space automatically.
- Traffic Weaver: If the player lands on a "Car/Traffic" Grey Spot, they take zero pollution damage.

### Reg the Scientist
**I can see the invisible!**

Abilities:

- Forecasting: Twice per game, the player can click a "Sensor Button" to reveal the correct answer to a difficult Quiz Card.

### Tommy the Ranger
**Trees are the lungs of the Earth.**

Abilities:

- Fire Resistance: If the player lands on a "Wildfire" spot, they do NOT take pollution damage.
- Photosynthesis Boost: When the Ranger lands on a "Plant Trees" (Green) spot, they remove double pollution.
